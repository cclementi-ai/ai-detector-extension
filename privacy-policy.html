AI Writing Detector - Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 29, 2026

What data we collect

AI Writing Detector collects student submission text and document metadata (title, word count) submitted by teachers for analysis. This data is stored on secure servers to enable baseline comparisons.

How we use data

Collected text is used solely to analyze writing patterns and compare against teacher-verified baselines. Data is never sold or shared with third parties.

Google API data

When teachers connect their Google account, we access Google Docs content and Drive activity history solely to perform writing analysis. We do not store Google account credentials.

Data retention

Submission data is retained until deleted by the teacher or school administrator.
